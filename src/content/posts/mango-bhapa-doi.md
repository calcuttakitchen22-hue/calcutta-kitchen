## Ingredients

- 1 cup hung curd
- 1/2 cup condensed milk
- 1/4 cup mango pulp
- 2 tbsp warm milk
- Kesar
- 1 elaichi

## Method

Mix all ingredients until smooth.

Pour into moulds.

Steam until set.

Cool completely and refrigerate.

## Mango Syrup

Cook mango pulp, sugar and water until a smooth syrup forms.

Cool before serving.

## Serving

Top the chilled bhapa doi with mango syrup and serve cold.
